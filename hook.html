    At Violet

    We believe that digital art has the potential to revolutionize the art world, but traditional barriers such as high costs and exclusivity have limited its reach. By providing a free platform for artists to showcase their work and for collectors to discover and purchase NFTs, we are breaking down those barriers and creating a more inclusive and equitable art world.

    As for why users should make NFTs, the answer is simple: NFTs are transforming the way we think about art, ownership, and value. By creating an NFT, an artist can prove ownership and authenticity of their work on the blockchain, ensuring that it can't be duplicated or forged. This provides a level of security and trust that is often lacking in the digital art world.

    Set up your wallet

    To start collecting and trading NFTs on Violet, you'll need to sign up your cryptocurrency wallet. Signing up your wallet is quick and easy.

    Create your collection

    Creating a collection on Violet is a great way to showcase your unique taste and interests in the world of digital art. Whether you're a seasoned collector or just starting out.

    Add your NFTs

    Adding your NFTs to Violet is easy and straightforward. Whether you're an artist looking to sell your work, or a collector looking to showcase your unique collection, Violet offers a user-friendly platform for adding and managing your NFTs.

    List art for sale

    Listing your NFTs for sale on Violet is a great way to monetize your digital assets and connect with buyers from around the world. Plus, with Violet's secure and user-friendly platform, you can buy and sell with confidence.
