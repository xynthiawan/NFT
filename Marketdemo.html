    Snowfro

    Erick, aka Snowfro, is an NFT artist and the founder of Artblocks.io. As an artist, he has minted one curated project created by himself, the “Chromie Squiggle”, so far.

    Rainbow Squiggles is an abstract art piece that captivates the viewer with its fluid and dynamic form. The focal point of the piece is a single, undulating line that twists and turns in a seemingly spontaneous way, creating a sense of movement and energy. The line is composed of vibrant rainbow hues, which draw the eye and add to the piece's playful and joyful mood.

    At first glance, the piece appears to be a simple squiggle, but upon closer inspection, the complexity of the line becomes apparent. The line is composed of countless smaller curves and waves, each one subtly different from the last. This gives the piece a sense of depth and texture, and encourages the viewer to explore the intricate details of the line.

    Nyan Cat is a YouTube video uploaded in April 2011, which became an internet meme. The video merged a Japanese pop song with an animated cartoon cat with a Pop-Tart for a torso flying through space and leaving a rainbow trail behind. The video ranked at number five on the list of most viewed YouTube videos in 2011.
