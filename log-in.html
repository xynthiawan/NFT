    ABOUT

    Just as the gemstone needs to be brought into the light to be appreciated, our digital art needs to be seen and valued. But unlike traditional art that is displayed in galleries and museums, digital art is often overlooked in the vastness of the internet. We believe that every piece of digital art is like a gemstone in the dark, waiting to be discovered and appreciated.

    That's why we created Violet, a platform that brings together the most exceptional digital art and showcases it in all its glory. Our website is purple, a color that symbolizes creativity, luxury, and sophistication. It represents the beauty and value of the digital art that we offer, just like a gemstone in the light.
