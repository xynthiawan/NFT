    Account

    At Violet, we understand that the needs of our users can differ depending on whether they are artists or collectors. That's why we've designed our platform to provide a unique experience for each group, with separate accounts for authors and browsers.

    Set up your wallet

    To start collecting and trading NFTs on Violet, you'll need to sign up your cryptocurrency wallet.

    Create your collection

    To get started, simply log in to your author account and navigate to the "Collections" tab. From there, you can create a new collection by clicking the "Create Collection" button.

    Add your NFTs

    To add your NFTs, first log in to your author account and navigate to the "My NFTs" tab.

    List arts for sale

    You'll be asked to set a price for your NFT, either in cryptocurrency or in US dollars. You can also choose whether to sell your NFT as an auction, with bidding starting at a certain price, or as a fixed price sale.
